Who We Are

We are a student-led Junior Advisory Board that supports Assist ME’s mission of providing free mobility equipment to individuals with low incomes or little to no insurance.

Our members help through cleaning events, interview phone calls, data projects, and social media content. Together, we make it easier for people to move safely from hospital to home and back into community life.

What We Do

Junior Advisory Board members support Assist ME through hands-on service and leadership opportunities.

Cleaning Events

Clean and prepare wheelchairs, walkers, and other equipment at Assist ME’s locations in Myers Park and Monroe.

Interview Phone Calls

Call past equipment recipients to learn how Assist ME has impacted their lives and gather stories.

Data Projects

Help organize and analyze data to improve Assist ME’s operations and impact reporting.

Marketing Projects

Create social media content and outreach materials to share Assist ME’s mission with the community.
