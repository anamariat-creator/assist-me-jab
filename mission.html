Our Mission

Supporting Assist ME through service, leadership, and community impact.

Assist ME’s Mission

Assist ME provides free, essential mobility equipment to individuals with low incomes or little to no insurance, helping them move safely from hospital to home or back into community life with dignity and independence.

By refurbishing and distributing wheelchairs, walkers, and other equipment, Assist ME ensures that mobility is not limited by financial barriers. The Junior Advisory Board amplifies this mission by volunteering, gathering stories, and supporting data and marketing efforts.

How the Junior Advisory Board Helps

Hands-On Service

Students participate in cleaning events, preparing equipment so it’s safe, clean, and ready for new recipients.

Storytelling & Interviews

Interview phone calls help capture the real impact of Assist ME on individuals and families.

Data & Outreach

Data and marketing projects help Assist ME grow, secure support, and reach more people in need.
