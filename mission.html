Assist ME’s Mission

Assist ME is a nonprofit that provides free, essential mobility equipment to individuals with low incomes or little to no insurance to help them move safely from hospital to home or from home back into community life with dignity and independence.

Learn More

Our Role as JAB

We are a student-led group that helps Assist ME’s cause. We do this through cleaning events, interviewing past equipment recipients, working on data projects, and filming social media content.
