Join Our Junior Advisory Board

Build leadership skills while making a real impact in your community.

Why Join?

  • Develop leadership and communication skills.
  • Gain hands-on experience in service and nonprofit work.
  • Build a strong resume and college applications.
  • Collaborate with peers across schools and communities.

Requirements

  • Must be a high school student.
  • Reliable, proactive, and eager to contribute.
  • Commit to at least 10 hours of service per year.

Logistics

Cleaning events are held in Myers Park and Monroe, typically a 30–45 minute drive. Interview calls, social media content, and data projects can be completed remotely.
